I am Mai Delgado. I am fascinated about nature and everything in it. See the colors of peacock feathers, of zinnia petals, of mayana leaves? Isn't it wonderful to wake up by the beach and zip a cup of coffee? Don't you get ecstatic to see the beautiful mountains and much more to conquer it?

That's me. What I am most fascinated about is how the human mind and human hands work. Man is capable of creating wonderful things. And in this century, human creativity has gone to overwhelming heights - most especially, with the aid of technology.

That part -- technology, is where I am more curious about. It is most useful, but at the same time, dangerous. I am yet at the start of my journey. To where this would lead me? I know not where, --and how..and yet..
